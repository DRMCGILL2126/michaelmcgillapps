Frequently Asked Questions

PropaneScout

Q: How are propane prices determined?

Prices are reported by users in the community. We do not set or guarantee any prices shown in the app.

Q: Why don't I see any locations near me?

PropaneScout relies on community-reported data. If your area doesn't have reports yet, you can be the first to contribute!

Alarmony

Q: Why isn't my alarm playing music?

Make sure you have an active Apple Music subscription and that the app has permission to access Apple Music in Settings > Alarmony.

Custom Interval Workout Timer

Q: Does the timer work on Apple Watch?

Yes! The Apple Watch companion app mirrors your timer and provides haptic alerts on your wrist.

General

Q: How do I restore my purchases?

Go to the app's settings and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID or Google account used for the original purchase.

Q: How do I delete my data?

All app data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently remove all data.
