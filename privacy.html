Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all apps published by Michael McGill, including Layman Bible, Alarmony, Custom Interval Workout Timer, PropaneScout, and any future apps ("our Apps").

Introduction

We respect your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile applications.

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

Our Apps store user preferences, settings, and content locally on your device only. This may include:

  • App settings and preferences
  • Bookmarks, highlights, notes, and reading progress
  • Alarm settings, timer configurations, or other app-specific data

This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated otherwise.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Health or financial data

Advertising

Some of our Apps display ads provided by third-party advertising networks (such as Google AdMob). These services may collect:

  • Device Identifiers: Advertising ID (IDFA) for ad personalization
  • Usage Data: General interaction data for ad relevance

You can limit ad tracking in your device's Settings > Privacy > Tracking. If you opt out, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant.

In-App Purchases and Subscriptions

Some of our Apps offer optional in-app purchases or subscriptions. All payment processing is handled by Apple through the App Store or Google through Google Play. We do not have access to your payment details, credit card information, or account credentials.

Third-Party Services

Our Apps may integrate with third-party services (such as Apple Music, Apple Podcasts, text-to-speech engines, or Firebase). These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

Children's Privacy

Our Apps do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Our Apps do not require account creation or personal data submission.

Data Security

Since most data is stored locally on your device, it is protected by your device's built-in security features. If you delete an App, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete all App data by uninstalling the App
  • Manage permissions through your device settings
  • Opt out of ad personalization through your device settings
  • Cancel subscriptions through your Apple ID or Google Play account settings

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
