PropaneScout FAQ

Do I need to sign in to use PropaneScout?

▼

Google Sign-In is optional. You can browse and view propane prices near you without creating an account. However, signing in allows you to report prices and appear on the community leaderboard.

Why does the app need my location?

▼

Location access is used to find propane prices near you on the interactive map. You can deny location permission and browse by searching for specific stores manually. All location data is used only within the app and is not sold to third parties.

How do I report a price?

▼

To report a price: Tap the "+" button, select the store location, enter the price you paid, select the propane type (tank exchange, refill, or new tank), and submit. Your report helps the community find the best deals!

Custom Interval Workout Timer FAQ

How do I create a custom timer?

▼

Tap the "Create Timer" or "+" button on the home screen. Add intervals by entering the duration, adding a custom name, and selecting a color. You can chain multiple timers together for complex multi-part workouts. Save your timers for future use.

Does it work with Apple Watch?

▼

Yes! A dedicated Apple Watch companion app is included with your purchase. You can start and control workouts directly from your wrist, making it perfect for tracking intervals during training.

Does it sync with Apple Health?

▼

Yes, completed workouts can be logged to Apple Health automatically. Your workout data integrates seamlessly with your health and fitness records.

Alarmony FAQ

How do I set an alarm with my music?

▼

Create a new alarm, set your desired wake-up time, and tap on the alarm sound selector. Choose "Music" and select a song from your device's music library. Your chosen song will play at the alarm time.

Can I use Spotify or Apple Music?

▼

The app works with your local music library on your device, including songs purchased from iTunes or synced to your iPhone. Downloaded songs from Spotify or Apple Music can be used as alarm sounds.
